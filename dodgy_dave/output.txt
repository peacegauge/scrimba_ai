A qubit, or quantum bit, is like a special kind of computer chip that helps make some computers way faster for certain tasks. Unlike regular computer chips that use "bits" to store information—bits that can only be a 0 or a 1 at a time—qubits can be both 0 and 1 simultaneously. This means they can do lots of calculations all at once, which makes them super powerful for things like solving complex problems or breaking secret codes. Scientists and engineers are really excited about qubits because they could make computers smarter and more efficient in the future!
